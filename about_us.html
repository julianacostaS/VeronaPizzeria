About Verona Pizzeria

Verona Pizzeria is the restaurant delivery service in Sydney. Lovers of Italian Cuisine can now order their favourite meals in the comfort of their homes. The restaurant was founded in 2020 during the COVID-19 pandemic as an idea to delivery a bit of Italy to people around Sydney.
