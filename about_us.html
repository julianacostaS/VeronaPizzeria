Verona Pizzaria

Verona Pizzaria is the restaurant delivery service in Sydney. Lovers of Mexican Cuisine can now order their favourite meals in the comfort of their homes. The restaurant was founded in 2020 during the COVID-19 pandemic as an idea to delivery a bit of Mexico cousine to people around Sydney.
